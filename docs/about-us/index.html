About Us

Research. Propose.
Debate. Create.
For innovation.

HARMONY連 (read "HARMONY REN") is an Open Innovation Collective founded in Italy, focused on research, discussion, and innovation.

How does the collective work?

There are three main kinds of members:

  • Debater - Open to everyone. Can read, write, submit, and review theses.
    Contributes to discussion and development but holds no legal or financial responsibilities.
  • Core Member - A legally recognized role. Holds the same abilities as a Debater, plus the right to earn income and assume legal responsibilities within the collective.
  • Proxy - A member who represents a legal entity that wants to work with HARMONY連.

To get involved with us, please refer to Get Involved.
