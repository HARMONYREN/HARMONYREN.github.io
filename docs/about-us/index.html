About Us

HARMONY連 (read "HARMONY REN") is an Open Innovation Collective founded in Italy, focused on research, discussion, and innovation.

How does the collective work?

There are three main kinds of members:
Debater: Open to everyone. Can read, write, submit, and review theses. Contributes to discussions and development but holds no legal or financial responsibilities.
Core Member: A legally recognized role. Holds the same abilities as a Debater, plus the right to earn income and assume legal responsibilities within the collective.
Proxy: An individual who represents a legal entity that wants to work with HARMONY連.

To get involved with us, please refer to Get Involved.

Purpose of Theses

Theses are documents designed to present ideas in a rational, structured manner. Each thesis should include:

  • A clear proposal.
  • Its corresponding antithesis, outlining potential objections or counterpoints.
  • An explanation of why the proposal matters.
  • A high-level description of how it can be implemented (detailed elaborations can follow in subsequent work).

Each thesis must be cryptographically signed, which can be done using 所ID.
Note: The signature must be submitted as a separate binary file.

If the thesis is produced collaboratively:

  • List all contributors in the authors section along with their IDs.
  • Include each contributor’s cryptographic signature and key ID when submitting the thesis.
